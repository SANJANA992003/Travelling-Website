ABOUT US

Cleartrip was founded in 2006 as a hotels and air aggregator by Stuart Crighton, Hrush Bhatt, and Matthew Spacie. Seeing the fragmentation that existed in the Indian travel and hospitality sector, the founders decided to enter the online travel space.

Cleartrip also operates Cleartrip for Business, an online corporate travel management tool; Agent Box, a travel tool for travel agents; and Cleartrip Mobile,a travel booking app for mobile devices. In 2012, it launched new services such as Quickeys, a hotel booking application and other journey planning services.

DESTINATION

We all have known Nainital as a hill station, being a summer tourist destination. It is also surrounded by areas that have witnessed real estate growth. Bhowali, in Nainital district of Uttarakhand, is one such destination that is a gateway to many other places of interest across the state. With just 11 km away from Nainital, Bhowali is calmer and the property prices are lower, comparatively.

Alwar, a city of Rajasthan, is the centre point from those who are travelling from Delhi to Jaipur. Jaipur, as we all know, witnesses a huge footfall from not just international soil but is also a weekend destination for many living in the national capital region. Alwar, on the other hand a residential spot which boasts of a beautiful lake and picturesque valley of the Aravalli range, (160 Km away) is favourite among affluent buyers. A lesser known tourist destination, Alwar has caught the attention of developers to build few prominent projects
Lonavala, Khandala, Baneshwar and Alibaug are a few weekend destination preferred by people of Mumbai and Pune. But, one more destination, which is being preferred as a better locality for property investment is Ratnagiri. It is already popular as a port city in Maharashtra. Salasar Life Spaces The Riverview, Chaitanya Developers See Vista Villas, Paranjape Schemes Janardan Plaza and Oceanic Valley are few of the prominent projects.

The demand for the second home is booming in Panchkula as it makes for a viable economic investment for the buyer as well as the developers due to low construction charge and easy accessibility of labour and construction material. Lying on the foothills of Himalayas, Panchkula is catching the eyeballs for many well-off buyers. DLF Valley, Chinar Group Chinar Apartments and Trishla Plus Homes are few of the projects in this locality.
